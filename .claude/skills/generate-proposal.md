# Skill: Generate Proposal
# AIMarketer.co Agency Operations System

## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Generates a complete client proposal from a brief. Called by
the proposal-writer agent. Produces either a short platform
proposal (Upwork, Contra, PeoplePerHour) or a full direct
client proposal depending on the brief.

## WHEN TO USE THIS SKILL
Use when:
- Responding to a job post on a freelance platform
- Sending a direct proposal to a warm lead
- A qualified lead has been scored 70+ by lead-qualifier

## PRE-WRITING CHECKLIST
Before writing, confirm you have:
[ ] Client name or job title
[ ] Platform (Upwork / Contra / PeoplePerHour / direct)
[ ] Their core problem (one sentence)
[ ] Service to lead with
[ ] Closest portfolio proof point
[ ] Budget or pricing tier (if known)

## PROPOSAL TYPE DECISION

### Use SHORT PROPOSAL when:
- Platform is Upwork, Contra, or PeoplePerHour
- Word limit applies (under 300 words)
- Speed of response matters

### Use FULL PROPOSAL when:
- Direct client outreach
- Lead score is 90+
- Client has requested a formal proposal
- Deal value is above R15,000 / $900

## SHORT PROPOSAL STRUCTURE
(Under 300 words — for freelance platforms)

LINE 1: [Option A, B, or C from outreach formula]

PARAGRAPH 1: Their problem in their language (2-3 sentences)

PARAGRAPH 2: One portfolio metric closest to their case
(1-2 sentences — metric first, process second)

PARAGRAPH 3: What you will deliver (3-5 bullet points
with specifics — numbers where possible)

CLOSING: One diagnostic question + one CTA

## FULL PROPOSAL STRUCTURE
(For direct clients)

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
   Their problem + estimated revenue or time impact
   (3-4 sentences)

2. WHAT I UNDERSTAND ABOUT YOUR SITUATION
   Specific observations — not generic
   (3-5 sentences)

3. WHAT I WILL BUILD FOR YOU
   | Deliverable | Format | Timeline | Metric |
   (table format)

4. HOW IT WORKS
   Plain English explanation of the AI system
   No jargon. No acronyms without explanation.

5. PROOF OF CAPABILITY
   3 metrics from portfolio most relevant to this client

6. INVESTMENT
   | Service | What's included | Investment |
   (table format)

7. NEXT STEP
   One action only — book a 20-minute scoping call
   or reply to confirm scope
