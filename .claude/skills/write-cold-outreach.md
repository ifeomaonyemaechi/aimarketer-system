# Skill: Write Cold Outreach
# Ifeoma Automation Operations System

## WHAT THIS SKILL DOES
Generates a complete cold outreach message for a single lead
across one platform. Called by the outreach-writer agent.
Every message must reference something specific about the
prospect's brand — no generic templates.

## WHEN TO USE THIS SKILL
Use when:
- A qualified lead needs initial outreach
- A job post needs a response
- A brand has been identified as a strong fit

## PRE-WRITING CHECKLIST
Before writing, confirm you have:
[ ] Lead name and brand name
[ ] Platform (email / LinkedIn / Instagram / DM)
[ ] One specific observation about their brand
  (recent post, product launch, content gap, tool used)
[ ] Closest portfolio proof point to their use case
[ ] Service to lead with

## THE OBSERVATION RULE
Every outreach message must open with or reference a
specific, verifiable observation about the prospect:

Good observations:
- "Saw your SPF50 facial oil drop three days ago"
- "Noticed you went quiet for 6 days right when the
  belt dropped"
- "Your daily posts deserve better than 2% engagement"
- "That blog feature deserved more than one day"

Bad observations (too generic):
- "I came across your brand and love what you do"
- "Your Instagram presence caught my attention"
- "I've been following your journey"

## EXECUTION INSTRUCTIONS
1. Confirm platform and lead details from brief
2. Identify the single strongest observation
3. Choose the correct first line option (A, B, or C)
4. Write paragraph 1 — their problem in their language
5. Write paragraph 2 — one metric from portfolio
6. Write paragraph 3 — 3-5 specific deliverables
7. Write closing — one question + one CTA
8. Check word count for platform
9. Run quality checklist
10. Save to outputs/AIMARKETER_OUTREACH_[BRAND]_[YYYYMMDD].md

## WORD COUNT LIMITS
- Cold email: 150-200 words
- LinkedIn InMail: 200-300 words
- Instagram DM: 100-150 words
- Twitter/X DM: under 100 words

## QUALITY GATES
Before saving output, confirm:
[ ] Opening observation is specific and verifiable
[ ] No banned agency words used
[ ] One portfolio metric referenced
[ ] One diagnostic question in closing
[ ] Under word count for platform
[ ] Sounds like a person, not a pitch

## OUTPUT STRUCTURE

Brand: [brand name]
Platform: [platform]
Observation used: [the specific thing referenced]

[full outreach message]
