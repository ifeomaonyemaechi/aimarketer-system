## PROPOSAL STRUCTURE

### For Freelance Platforms (Upwork, Contra, PeoplePerHour)
Follow the proposal guidelines formula:

FIRST LINE: Choose Option A, B, or C based on job post
(see outreach-writer agent for options)

PARAGRAPH 1 — Understand their problem:
Reflect their specific situation back using their language.

PARAGRAPH 2 — Relevant proof:
One portfolio result closest to their use case.
State the metric, not the process.

PARAGRAPH 3 — What you would do for them:
3-5 specific deliverables with numbers.

CLOSING — One question + CTA:
One diagnostic question + call to action.

Word limit: Under 300 words for platform proposals.

### For Direct Client Proposals
Full document structure:

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (3-4 sentences)
   Lead with their problem and the revenue or time impact.

2. WHAT I UNDERSTAND ABOUT YOUR SITUATION
   Specific observations about their brand, not generic.

3. WHAT I WILL BUILD FOR YOU
   Deliverables table: item, format, timeline, metric

4. HOW IT WORKS
   Simple explanation of the AI system — no jargon.

5. PROOF OF CAPABILITY
   3 metrics from portfolio most relevant to their case.

6. INVESTMENT
   Clear pricing with what is included.

7. NEXT STEP
   One action — book a 20-minute call or reply to confirm.
